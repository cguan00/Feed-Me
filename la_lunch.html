1. Malibu Farm

Address: 23000 Pacific Coast Hwy , Malibu, CA 90265

Star Rating: 4 / 5

Website link: https://www.malibu-farm.com/pier-cafe/

Rustic-chic bistro with an oceanfront deck serving farm-to-table New American

fare & local wine.

2. Joan's on Third

Address: 8350 W 3rd St Los Angeles, CA 90048

Star Rating: 4 / 5

Website link: https://joansonthird.com/

Joan's on Third is a perfect stop for convenient gourmet meals to take home,

tote to work or enjoy on the patio. This hotspot is reminiscent of a bustling

market place, complete with crowds of folks hurrying to get items that are

likely to sell out. Using only high-quality, fresh ingredients, Joan's offers

sophisticated takes on old favorites, such as meatloaf sandwiches, macaroni and

cheese and tuna salad.

3. The Butcher’s Daughter

Address: 1205 Abbot Kinney Blvd Venice, CA 90291

Star Rating: 4 / 5

Website link: https://www.thebutchersdaughter.com/

Bright, bleached-wood outpost offering vegetarian (mostly vegan) fare & juices

in a relaxed space.

4. Pink’s Hot Dogs

Address: 709 N La Brea Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90038

Star Rating: 4.4 / 5

Website link: http://www.pinkshollywood.com/

Creatively topped dogs (some named for celebrities) draw long lines at

this historic roadside spot.

5. Loqui

Address: 8830 Washington Blvd Ste 104 Culver City, CA 90232

Star Rating: 4.6 / 5

Website link: https://www.eatloqui.com/
