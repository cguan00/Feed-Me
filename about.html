Feed Me

About

Hello! Welcome to Feed Me! Feed Me is a website that allows its users find different food places near their location in Los Angeles, California.

You can choose from a variety of restaurants that specialize in breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner, and dessert. On this website, you can find lists

of different restaurants with their address, rating (based on google), and website. You can also click on the title of the restaurants that leads

you to a different individual page containing more information about the restaurant.
